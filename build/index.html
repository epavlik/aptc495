Interests

My first hobby is picking up hobbies. I have taught myself music composition, fiction writing, photography, programming, web development, linguistics, modeling of objects for use in 3D printing, and a host of others. My ability and willingness to learn new skills is one of my greatest assets.

My great loves are the fiber arts: knitting, crochet, macrame. I began knitting in 2017, crocheting in 2018, and most recently picked up macrame. I have made blankets, toys, bags, scarves, hats, and sweaters. I really enjoy crocheting doilies, as they are very technically challenging and require dexterity and attention to detail.

My other great passion is meteorology in general, and tornadoes in specific. Since 2012, I have served as a volunteer storm spotter with the Skywarn program through the National Weather Service, reporting observed severe weather phenomena such as flooding, damaging wind, hail, and funnel clouds/tornadoes.

I am a semi-verbal, lower-needs autistic woman. Consequently, writing is my primary form of communication, and I have learned to use it to great effect over the years. Much of my spare time is dedicated to both self-advocacy and advocating for others in the community. My current area of interest is autism in marginalized and historically underprivileged demographics.
